Our CEO Wins Ghana C.E.O & Industrial Awards 2025

Home / News / CEO Award 2025

We are thrilled to announce that our CEO, Suallah Adams Joudar, has been honored with the prestigious Ghana C.E.O & Industrial Awards 2025! This remarkable achievement is a testament to his visionary leadership, dedication, and the positive impact he continues to make in the business and philanthropic landscape of Ghana.

Our CEO, Suallah Adams Joudar, was recognized for outstanding leadership, innovation, and commitment to driving growth and transformation in the industry. This award is a proud moment for the entire SAJ Foundation family and all our partners, volunteers, and supporters.

Impactful Leadership

Under the guidance of Suallah Adams Joudar, SAJ Foundation has expanded its reach, launched impactful community programs, and inspired a new generation of changemakers. This award fuels our passion to continue transforming lives and building a brighter future for all.

Gratitude & Next Steps

We extend our heartfelt congratulations to our CEO, Suallah Adams Joudar, and express our gratitude to everyone who has supported our mission. Together, we are making a difference!
