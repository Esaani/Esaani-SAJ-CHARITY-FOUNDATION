Join Our Volunteer Team

Make a difference in your community by volunteering with SAJ Foundation. Your time and skills can help transform lives and create lasting positive change.

Volunteer Opportunities

Find the perfect way to contribute your time and talents

Community Feeding

Help prepare and serve nutritious meals to families in need during our weekly feeding programs.

2-4 hours/week Various locations

Educational Support

Provide tutoring, mentoring, and educational support to children in our community programs.

3-6 hours/week Community centers

Health Outreach

Assist with health screenings, education programs, and mobile clinic operations.

4-8 hours/month Mobile locations

Event Support

Help organize and run community events, fundraisers, and awareness campaigns.

Flexible hours Various venues

Administrative Support

Provide office support, data entry, social media management, and other administrative tasks.

2-5 hours/week Office/Remote

Skills Training

Share your professional skills by teaching workshops in areas like crafts, technology, or business.

2-4 hours/session Training centers

Ready to Make a Difference?

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and help us create positive change in communities across Ghana. We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and skill levels.

Benefits of Volunteering

  • Make a meaningful impact in your community
  • Gain valuable experience and skills
  • Meet like-minded people and build connections
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your availability
  • Receive training and support
  • Recognition and appreciation for your contributions
